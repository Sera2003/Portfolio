FitMate – Cross-Platform Fitness UI/UX

A complete UI/UX system for a fitness application available on Web, Android, and iOS. The design focuses on clean navigation, progress tracking, personalized workouts, and a consistent look-and-feel across all platforms.

Project Information

  • Category: UI/UX Design
  • Platforms: Web, Android, iOS
  • Tools: Figma, Illustrator
  • Deliverables: 39 Screens (13 per platform)
  • Role: UI/UX Designer

Design Goals

  • Provide a consistent experience across web and mobile platforms.
  • Make workouts, nutrition and progress tracking easy to access in 1–2 taps.
  • Use a calming, energetic color palette suitable for a fitness brand.
  • Support smartwatch integration and wellness monitoring for advanced users.

UX Flow Overview

The experience starts with a simple login/sign-up flow, then leads users into a personalized dashboard where they can track workouts, view upcoming sessions, follow nutrition plans, and monitor wellness metrics. Navigation patterns are kept similar across platforms so users can switch between devices without confusion.

Platform Highlights

Android

  • Material-style bottom navigation with quick access to Home, Workouts, and Nutrition.
  • Workout cards with difficulty level, time, and coach information.
  • Nutrition page showing daily macros and meal suggestions.

iOS

  • Coach screen with highlighted active program and upcoming sessions.
  • Community page for posts, likes, and challenges.
  • Smartwatch and wellness screens focused on heart rate and activity tracking.

Web

  • Large dashboard layout highlighting progress, active plans, and quick actions.
  • Workout explorer with filters by level, duration, and equipment.
  • Progress page with charts for weight, workouts completed, and streaks.
