AquaLounge & Bistro – 3D Animated Scene

A two-story 3D environment created in Autodesk 3ds Max, combining an aquarium lounge on the first floor with a restaurant and bar on the second floor. The scene focuses on relaxation, social interaction, and atmospheric lighting through detailed modeling, materials, and animation.

Project Information

  • Category: 3D Modeling & Animation
  • Software: Autodesk 3ds Max
  • Scene Type: Interior & Exterior Environment
  • Focus: Lighting, Atmosphere, and Animation

Concept & Goal

The project was designed as a calming yet social environment where visitors can either relax with an aquarium view or enjoy food and drinks in a vibrant bistro setting. The two floors balance stress relief and social experiences in one unified 3D scene. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Scene Breakdown

Exterior Environment

  • Underwater-inspired exterior with fish, sharks, an octopus, and colorful corals.
  • Added grass and sky elements to create a rich, immersive environment outside the building.

First Floor – Aquarium Lounge

  • Large aquarium as the main visual focus for relaxation and stress relief.
  • Cozy seating areas designed for enjoying coffee in a peaceful atmosphere.
  • Bookshelf and decor elements to enhance the warm, calm feeling of the space.
  • Staircase leading to the second floor, connecting lounge and bistro areas smoothly.

Second Floor – Restaurant & Bar

  • Stylish dining area with arranged furniture for comfortable social gatherings.
  • Dedicated dance floor for nightlife and entertainment.
  • Bar area with red lighting accents, whiskey bottles, and cigars for a chic, premium vibe.

Lighting & Cameras

  • Multiple cameras placed in each scene (exterior, lounge, and bistro) for clean renders and animations.
  • Soft white lighting on the first floor to keep the aquarium and lounge relaxing.
  • Warm lighting on the second floor to highlight the restaurant experience.
  • Red accent lights at the bar to create a bold, modern atmosphere.
